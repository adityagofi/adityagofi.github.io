Company Profile
Motion Graphics

As a Freelance Video Editor & Motion Designer, I worked on several company profile video projects for clients including Mantappu Corp, BEM FMIPA Universitas Brawijaya 2021, and AAI (Asosiasi Aktuaris Indonesia). In each project, I was responsible for the full post-production process, including editing, motion graphics, sound design, and visual storytelling.

I collaborated closely with clients to ensure the video structure, tone, and visual elements effectively communicated their organizational values, mission, and identity. Using tools like Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects, I delivered professional and compelling profile videos tailored for various use cases—from corporate branding and internal presentations to social media and formal introductions.
